How to Play

  1. You can choose to play a card from your hand by placing it face up on the top of the Discard Pile and then follow the instructions on the card. Alternatively, you have the option to refrain from playing any cards, which is also acceptable.
  2. Once you've followed the instructions on a card, you have the opportunity to play additional cards. There's no limit to the number of cards you can play, so feel free to play as many as you wish.
  3. To conclude your turn, draw a card from the Draw Pile into your hand, and cross your fingers that it isn't an Exploding Kitten.

Rules

You'll have a deck of cards containing Exploding Kittens. You play the game by putting the deck face down and taking turns drawing cards until someone draws an Exploding Kitten.
